📋 CSV Format Guide

Your file must have a header row with the column names shown. Values should match the training data exactly:

  • Gender: Male or Female
  • Married: Yes or No
  • Dependents: 0, 1, 2, 3+
  • Education: Graduate or Not Graduate
  • Self_Employed: Yes or No
  • ApplicantIncome: monthly income in dollars
  • LoanAmount: amount in thousands
  • Credit_History: 1 (good) or 0 (bad)
  • Property_Area: Urban, Semiurban, Rural

💡 Tips

The output CSV will include three new columns:

  • Predicted_Status: Y or N
  • Approved: Yes or No
  • Confidence_Pct: 0–100%
